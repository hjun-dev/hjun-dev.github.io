### **Project Overview**
* **Problem:** Traditional PID controllers struggle with the nonlinear dynamics of landing rockets.
* **Approach:** Implemented Sequential Convex Programming (SCP) to optimize trajectory in real-time.
* **Result:** Achieved 95% landing success rate in perturbed environments.

### **Tech Stack**
`Python` `Simulink` `CVXPY` `ROS2`
